# Privacy Policy: GenZ Academy

Your privacy is paramount. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

## 1. Data Collection
- We collect your name, email, and social profiles to build your professional identity.
- We track your solution history to provide accuracy and ranking metrics.

## 2. Code Privacy
- Your code is executed in isolated environments.
- We do not share your proprietary logic with third parties, except for AI analysis (OpenAI/Gemini) if requested by you via the "Explain" or "Review" features.

## 3. Cookies & Tracking
- We use JWT tokens and short-lived cookies for session management.
- We do not use third-party tracking pixels for advertising.

## 4. Your Rights
- You can request data deletion at any time via the support channel.
- Account reset tools are available to clear your submission history.
